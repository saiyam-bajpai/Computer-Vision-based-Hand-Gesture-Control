Drawing and holographic interaction layered on top of the original gesture engine
✦ New Feature
Spatial Canvas
Draw glowing neon strokes directly onto your live camera feed using nothing but your fingers. The canvas persists until you clear it.
1 Raise ring finger + pinky only to enter draw mode
2 Move your index fingertip to paint neon trails
3 Color auto-cycles through 5 vivid neon hues
4 Lower your hand or change gesture to stop drawing
✦ New Feature
Holo-Globe
Draw a circle in draw mode and watch it transform into an interactive holographic globe you can physically move with your palm.
1 Enter draw mode and trace a rough circle
2 Globe spawns at your circle's centre — all gestures pause
3 Hold palm near the globe to drag it across the screen
4 Globe dissolves after 4 s of no interaction, gestures resume
